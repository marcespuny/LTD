The participant becomes a Certified Exchange Trader upon passing all four 'series' exams on the Trader Program and gets referred to one of our Employment Partners.
We provide training for Industry Professionals, Business Tourists, Universities and Business Schools, Sixth Form Colleges and Private Individuals.

Program Core
The objective of the Trader Program at LTX is to prepare individuals to make strategic Trading-Investing and Research decisions in the Financial Industry.

- You will rotate around 20 active floor traders learning proven strategies, risk management techniques and trade plans of each individual trader.

- You will prepare for STA, CFA qualifications and FSA regulatory accreditation that you would require to participate actively in the Financial Industry.

- You will train to research Global Markets and present investment themes to Institutions according to standard industry procedures.

We are the only institution in the world that facilitates individuals to shadow active traders real-time on a live trading floor and learn from actual trading records using back-test strategies.

Employment Partners
LTX partners with leading Global Exchanges, Banks, Hedge Funds and Local Businesses all of which look to hire from the pool of talent accepted into our program.
