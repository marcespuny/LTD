International Trader Program


The International Trader Program is the only program of its sort in the world that facilitates individuals to shadow active traders real-time on a live trading floor.

The program is structured to cater exclusively for individuals looking to get an international exposure outside their comfort zones.
We only run this program at our flagship trading floor in London.

Program Core
The objective of the Trader Program at LTX is to prepare individuals to make strategic Trading-Investing and Research decisions in the Financial Industry.

- You will rotate around 20 active floor traders learning proven strategies, risk management techniques and trade plans of each individual trader.

- You will prepare for STA, CFA qualifications and FSA regulatory accreditation that you would require to actively participate in the Financial Industry.

- You will train to Research Global Markets and present investment themes to Institutions according to standard industry procedures.

We include an English Language module (Financial English) component on request

Why London?
The International Trader Program at the London Trading Exchange provides you with a unique and invaluable experience of trading on a live trading floor alongside some of the best floor traders in the industry.

Based in London (the heart of the global financial community) the experience offers you the privilege of access to distinctive resources such as wide networking opportunities with like-minded individuals, access to a deep pool of capital and other resources that will improve your global profile.

Joining the International Trader Program gives you a one year Category 5 membership to the London Trading Exchange where you will be able to access all the facilities at the state of the art trading floors in ten cities globally. This will enable you to Travel, Trade and Network.

. For more information visit Trading Floors.

Duration and Fees
The program lasts for 20 trading days of which your attendance could be flexible.
Full Program Fees including the English Language component are set at 1650 £.
See Program Structure

Certification
The participant becomes a Certified Exchange Trader upon passing all four ‘series’ exams on the Trader Program and gets referred to one of our Employment Partners.

Visa Requirements
Individuals from the countries listed below would not need a visa to travel to the United Kingdom.
New Zealand, Australia, Hong Kong , Singapore, Japan , South Korea, Malaysia, Taiwan, Brunei, Botswana, Namibia, United States of America, Canada, Austria , Greece, Netherlands, Belgium, Hungary, Norway, Bulgaria, Iceland, Poland, Cyprus , Ireland, Portugal, Czech Republic, Italy, Romania, Denmark , Latvia, Slovakia, Estonia, Liechtenstein, Slovenia, Finland, Lithuania, Spain, France, Luxembourg , Sweden, Germany , Malta , Mexico, Brazil, Chile, Argentina, Kuwait, Oman, UAE, Qatar, UAE.

Individuals from countries not listed above would have to request an invitation letter from the London Trading Exchange Group to apply for a visa.
