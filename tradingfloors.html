Trading Floors


LTX operates fully equipped start of the art trading floors in 10 cities globally.

Location of Trading Floors
London, Geneva, Singapore, Shanghai, Dubai, New York, Hong Kong, Mumbai, Johannesburg and Sao Paulo.

Our Trading Floors are conveniently located within business districts in all cities.
We accommodate requests from individuals (private investors and business travellers) to access the Prop Trading section.

Faculties
- Bespoke Professional Trading Desks
- Multiple Trading Platforms (TT, RTS, Stellar, PATS and Easyscreen)
- Ultra Low Latency DMA Network
- LTX Clear Clearing and Settlement
- Risk Management and Margin Finance Solutions
- In-house Market Analysis and Commentary
- Intra-day leverage
- Seed Capital for Alpha Traders
- Low cost commissions for high volume traders

The facilities provide a cost effective solution for private traders, investors and business travellers looking to trade, travel and network.

All members of the exchange have access to the floors in all 10 cities globally.
