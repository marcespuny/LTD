Events



Traders and members of The London Trading Exchange attend number of events both locally in London and internationally throughout the year.

There are three types of events held each month. Click on details for more information on the type of event, what to expect, time, location and profile of representatives.

Recruitment Event
We are looking for traders, managers and technical personnel to support our growing business.

Trading Workshop
This event is intended primary for individuals interested in trading capital markets and risk management.

End of month Evening Social
By invite only.

The LTX studio on the ground floor is a purpose-built event venue that can host up to 100 guests. It is ideal for corporate announcements, investor days and conferences.

For more information on attending an event or hiring the venue use the Contact Us page on this site or call the main switchboard on +44 (0)20 7476 9436.
