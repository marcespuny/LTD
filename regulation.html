Regulation


The role of the Regulation Department is to protect the integrity of LTX’s markets by ensuring trading is fair and transparent.

Today’s regulatory environment is complex and therefore we have put together a simplified Rulebook to help clients find the right information regarding Risk Management, Price and Data Connections, Formatting and Validation.
