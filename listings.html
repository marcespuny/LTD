Listings


London’s strategic position in the global financial community is the primary reason that attracts companies and investors to expose capital to markets via the exchange. The exchange offers the deepest and most liquid markets in the world.

We focus on infrastructure, liquidity and expertise as core principles in our growth mandate.

Criteria

1) Factors to consider: Does your company have?

2) Suitable Company Structure

3) Advice of key professionals such as lawyers and accountants

4) Reasons for listing

5) Strategic forward looking business plan

6) Required expertise of board, management, employees and other stakeholders

7) Robust financial/ trading track record

8) Awareness of time, cost and effort to list

9) Management team that is prepared for greater disclosure and openness

10) Management team committed to communicate

11) Shareholders prepared for loss of control

12) Management team committed to adhere to compliance and regulatory requirements

Considered timing of floatation to be beneficial for the company given market conditions

The company will need to promote itself successfully to persuade investor in London and around the world of its strengths to attract them to invest in the company and ensure the floatation is a success.

The company will need to demonstrate that the business is well managed and it is paramount for the company to have a strategic forward looking business plan and ensure that management has the right skills and commitment.


Process

Make an inquiry and we will guide you through the rest of the process.
1) Inquire
2) Meet with LTX
3) Appoint an issue manager
4) Allow for an assessment period
5) Pre-submission consultation
6) Complete submission
7) Approval
8)Provide prospectus
9) Build book, underwriting and balloting of shares
10) List

The company will need to file the required applications with relevant regulatory bodies and comply with EU and UK law at every stage of the listing process.

Government authorities will review all applications prior to granting administrative licenses.
The company will be required to produce certified financial statements for three full years of trading prior to meeting with us.

For more information on the listing process, regime, advice, choice of index, services, fees, regulation and documents please contact us using the information box on this page.
