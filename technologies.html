Technologies


FIX Marketplace Services
LTX is focused on reducing time to market and simplifying order flows using cutting edge technologies across all markets.
LTX uses FIX Marketplace Services as a primary platform shared with the NYSE.


Video

Video

This exclusive community consists of 700+ buy-sides, sell-sides, and vendors across the world that collectively makes up one of the industry’s broadest and deepest electronic trading communities.

List of solutions associated with FIX Marketplace include APPIA FIX, Order Routing and ioinet Liquidity Discovery.

Transactions are routed via Appia FIX Software, TradeScope and XILIX – Execution Management System (Asia) using Hosted Transactions Hub.

LTX Trading Connectivity is managed using Market Access Gateway (MAG) and BT Broadband Technologies.


International Trading services

International Order Book
LTX electronic order book for trading Global Depositary Receipts directly from LTX Clear.

European Quoting Service
LTX quote driven market making and trade reporting service for liquid MiFID securities not on another Exchange service.

European Trade Reporting
Trade reporting service for non-liquid MiFID securities not found on another Exchange service.

International Board
The electronic order book which allows member firms and their customers to trade constituents of the Straits Times Index, S&P Asia 50, S&P ASX, TAIEX, SET Index, PSE Index, VN-Index, Jakarta composite Index from London.


Trade Reporting

Parameters for technologies trade reporting set by LTX include:

- Daily trading schedule including timings
- Calculation of opening & closing prices
- Publication of settlement regime in operation
- Structure of price monitoring and ruling thresholds
- Order and trade types enabled including details of any minimum size constraints
- Comparison between Trading Services including selection criteria
- Overview of market maker obligation sizes
