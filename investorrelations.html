Overview
London Trading Exchange (LTX) is leading network of global exchanges that sits at the core of the world’s financial markets.

The Board of Directors of LTX sets high standards for the company’s employees, officers and directors. Embedded in this philosophy is the importance of sound corporate governance. It is the duty of the Board of Directors to serve as a prudent fiduciary for all stakeholders and to oversee management of the company’s business. To fulfil its responsibilities and to discharge its duty, the Board of Directors adhere to the following set procedures and standards.

- Open communication with our investors
- Efficient internal and external control mechanisms
- Loyal and trustful cooperation of Executive Board and Supervisory Board
- Transparent accounting
- Reporting in time

LTX is headquartered in London, United Kingdom and has Trading Floors in ten cities globally – London, Geneva, Singapore, Shanghai, Dubai, New York, Hong Kong, Mumbai, Johannesburg and Sao Paulo.

In 2012, LTX (formally fully owned by The London Trading Exchange and Clearing Group Ltd.) was partly acquired by Singapore based Commercial Universe Pte Ltd.

London Trading Exchange employees 600 people globally.
