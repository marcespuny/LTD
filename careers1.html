With an objective of connecting capital to markets - LTX provides high quality infrastructure, liquidity and expert solutions. In order to achieve the objective, we work in an atmosphere that encourages not only financial success but personal development.

We are looking for people that take ownership of their work and are able to see projects through to completion.

The Board of Directors at LTX view all employees, officers and directors as partners and are committed to providing an above average benefits remuneration package.

To make a direct application to LTX forward your CV to: recruitment@londontradingexchange.com or call the main switchboard on +44 (0)20 7476 9436.
